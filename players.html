About

A place to gather

The Players is a lively, welcoming sports bar and café created for the people who play, support and love sport. Whether you're coming off the tennis court, finishing a padel match, meeting your football team or simply popping in for coffee, this is your place to gather.

With live sports channels, comfortable indoor seating, an outdoor terrace, quality drinks and a relaxed café menu, The Players brings sport, food and community together under one roof.

A place for every player

Everyone is welcome

From club members and local teams to families, supporters and residents of Iver — whether you're here to compete, train, watch, eat, drink or unwind, you'll always find a warm welcome.
