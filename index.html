Our Story

A Century of Sport,
Community & Tradition

Richings Park Sports Club has been the heart of sport and community in Iver since 1926. Built on the pillars of heritage, authenticity and the enduring spirit of competition, the club offers an unrivalled environment for athletes, families and social members alike.

From immaculate grass tennis courts and panoramic padel arenas to our acclaimed 1920 Kitchen restaurant and luxury wellness retreat — RPSC is far more than a sports club. It is a way of life.
